About KCS Capital

Built from real operations.
Not from theory.

KCS Capital is the independent technology and research firm developing the infrastructure operated by 4orm Finance. Founded in Calgary. Built on operational discipline learned where execution is critical to health and life. Designed for the institutions that move Canadian capital.

Our Journey

Deep roots.
Real experience.

KCS Capital was built from real-world experience, not theory. Founders Chad, Sam, and Kevin grew up in family businesses where hard work, accountability, and integrity were not slogans. They were survival.

Years of building, managing, and scaling companies across industries revealed what entrepreneurs and families truly need: liquidity, protection, and stability that endures. That foundation, combined with a deep frustration over how fragile and centralized modern finance has become, sparked a mission to rebuild trust and sovereignty in the systems that shape our lives.

Bringing Humanity Back Into Finance

Innovation for people,
not for technology's sake.

Through the development of 4orm Finance, KCS Capital is designing Canada's first institution-grade Real World Asset Exchange and digital settlement network.

It is built for the institutions that move Canadian capital, banks, credit unions, custodians, and asset issuers, and for the people those institutions serve. Every element of our design emphasizes custody integrity, transparency, and accessibility, ensuring that participants can engage directly in governance and decision-making.

This is a return to human-centered finance, where trust is earned, privacy is protected, and power is shared.
